About Us

"FoodForNation" is a local hunger relief project operated by a group of volunteers in Bangladesh who are passionate about fighting hunger. Our goal is to integrate environmental and social responsibility in one objective. We collaborate with strategic partners-including food and non-food corporations-to collect food and deliver it to local non-profit partners in need by recovering surplus food from various partners, including supermarkets, farms, food retailers, restaurants, catering agencies and even rubbish bins. While we assist other non-profits, we also support impoverished areas by recycling food waste for reuse.

Food security does NOT just mean having access to food per se, but also being able to consume and utilize it in a way that ensures mental and physical health. We Tackle Food Waste
