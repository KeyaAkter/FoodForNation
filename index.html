About Us

FoodForNation is a local hunger relief project operated by a group of volunteers in Bangladesh who are passionate about fighting hunger. Our goal is to integrate environmental and social responsibility in one objective. We collaborate with strategic partners-including food and non-food corporations-to collect food and deliver it to local non-profit partners in need by recovering surplus food from events, arranging food drives, and fundraiser events. While we assist other non-profits, we also support impoverished areas by recycling food waste for reuse.
